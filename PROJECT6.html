Caribbean

click here
The Caribbean is a vibrant region known for its stunning islands,
rich cultural diversity, and historical significance, encompassing over 7,000 islands
and cays in the Caribbean Sea.
The Caribbean is located in the middle of the Americas,
centered around the Caribbean Sea and bordered by North America to the north,
Central America to the west,and South America to the south.
It comprises numerous islands, including the Greater Antilles (Cuba, Jamaica, Hispaniola, and Puerto Rico)
and the Lesser Antilles, as well as coastal areas of mainland countries like Mexico and Colombia.
The region is characterized by its diverse topography, featuring coral reefs, mountains, tropical forests,
and low-lying plantations.

Menaggio

click here
Menaggio, located on the western shore of Lake Como,
has a rich history that dates back to prehistoric times,
with significant Roman, medieval, and modern influences shaping its development.
In the 15th century a continual succession of wartime events predominated.
The Graubundener, who had already conquered the Valtellina,
kept Menaggio under pressure and in 1523 they succeeded defeating it and destroying its castle.
